ET

Eric Tymula, often referred to as E.T., was born right here in Danbury, CT. He moved to New Milford at young age and graduated from New Milford High School in 2006. Eric studied to be an electrician at Barren Institute of Technology and graduated with high honors in 2008.

In early 2005 Eric met Filipe Fernandes who was working at a local tattoo shop. This friendship sparked his interest in body art and was tattooed for the first time in 2006. Eric applied for an apprenticeship at the tattoo shop, and studied as an apprentice in both piercings and tattoos until he earned his spot on rotation. Eric spent the next five years tattooing and managing the shop his last few years.

Eric tattoos because he loves to see the look on people's faces after their piece is finished. Tattooing is a passion for Eric and enjoys doing all styles of art. "Black and grey" has become his favorite style but still likes to dive into anything that comes his way.
